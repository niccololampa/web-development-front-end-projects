    • A Timeline of Gen. Del Pilar's Life

    • November 1875 - Born in Bulacan Philippines
    • August 1896 - Graduated from Ateneo De Manila University. Revolution broke out and presented themselves for Military Service
    • December 1896 - Participated in the attack of Kakarong de Sili- Town of Pandi Bulacan
    • January 1897 - Among the defenders of Spanish counterattack which retook the Kakarong de Sili. Received a sligth bullet wound to the forehead. Promoted to Liutenant
    • August 1897 - Proposed and succesfully carried out attack on Paombong, Bulacan to capture weapons. Promoted to Lieutenant Colonel as aresult
    • December 1987 - Was exiled with the leaders of the revolution to Hong Kong as a result of the pact of Biak na Bato
    • May 1898 - Returned to the Philippine upon the break-out of Spanish- American War. Designated dictator of the province of Bulacan upon resumption of hostilities against colonial Spain
    • June 1898 - Accepted the Spanish surrender in Bulacan. Promote to Brigidaier General
    • February 1899 - Philippine American War broke out.
    • April 1899 - Led Filipino troops to a victory against American Major Franklin Bell in the Battle of Quingua
    • December 1899 - Died in Battle of Tirad Pass after serving as a rear-guard for Gen. Aguinaldo in a 5-hour stand-off against American troops led by Peyton C. March
